Your calendar for the month of october

Non-commercial events in Copenhagen

Evenio is a political project.
This calendar aims at making it easier to involve others and to get involved in the antiauthoritarian and extraparlamentarian left wing in Copenhagen!

Use it to advertise your events and mobilize people for your initiatives.

Events in may

Monday 1 10:30

Blockade Danish Defence League (DDL) - Antifascists from Copenhagen.
The DDL are trying to mobilize a 1. May celebration at Kongens Have in
Copenhagen. We will come and blockade their meeting. We meet at
Folkets Park 10:30.

13:00 - 18:00

“Work less, live more” - Antifacist 1. May
Demonstration from Nørrebrohallen to Folkets Park where there will be a
big street party. Allt åt Alla (Se), AFA, greek activists and Out of the Camps!
will speak.

14:00 - 17:30

“Regardless Government - Workers Struggle!”
Syndicalist 1. May. Demonstration from Gammeltorv.
